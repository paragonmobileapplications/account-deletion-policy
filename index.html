Account Deletion Policy

Developer Name: Paragon Mobile Applications

Welcome to our Account Deletion Policy page. Below you will find all the necessary steps to request account deletion.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, please send an email to:

paragon.mobile.applications@gmail.com

Include the following details in your email:

  • Your full name
  • Your registered email address
  • Reason for account deletion (optional)

Our team will process your request and confirm once your account has been deleted.

Data Deletion and Retention Policy

Upon account deletion, the following data will be removed:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email address).
  • Usage history and activity logs.

Data that will be retained for a specified duration includes:

  • Transaction records (retained for a period of 6 months as required by law).
  • Support tickets (retained for a period of 3 months for quality purposes).

After the retention period, all retained data will be permanently deleted.
